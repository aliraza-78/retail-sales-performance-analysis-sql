# Business Insights

## Sales Performance
- A small group of top customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue, indicating revenue concentration.
- Monthly sales show fluctuations, with some months performing below the overall average.

## Customer Behavior
- Several customers have not placed orders in the recent period, indicating potential customer churn.
- Average Order Value (AOV) provides insight into customer purchasing patterns and varies across orders.

## Product Analysis
- Certain products consistently rank at the top of sales within their categories.
- Some orders generate significantly higher revenue than average, highlighting high-value purchases.

## Business Takeaways
- Focus on retaining high-value customers while developing strategies to re-engage inactive customers.
- Investigate low-performing months and optimize promotions or inventory.
- Improve profitability by analyzing product-level performance.
